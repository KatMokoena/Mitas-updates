## What is IPMP?

IPMP is a comprehensive order and project management system that:

- **Tracks customer orders** from start to finish
- **Automatically calculates timelines** based on task dependencies and resource availability
- **Identifies critical paths** - showing which tasks directly impact deadlines
- **Monitors risk levels** - alerting us when projects are at risk of missing deadlines
- **Manages resources** - tracking staff assignments and equipment allocation
- **Provides real-time updates** - keeping everyone informed about project progress

## How It Works

1 Projects
Each project is entered into the system with a deadline. The system then automatically calculates when each task needs to be completed to meet that deadline. And an email will be sent with each step for clear communication and transperancy.

2 Tasks/Steps
Projects are broken down into individual tasks (or "steps"). Each task can:
- Have dependencies (some tasks must finish before others can start)
- Be assigned to specific team members
- Track estimated vs. actual time spent
- Show status: Not Started, In Progress, Completed, or Blocked

3 Automatic Timeline Calculation
The system's smart scheduling engine:
- Calculates when tasks should start and end
- Considers purchase delivery dates and resource availability
- Identifies the "critical path" - tasks that have no buffer time
- Automatically updates timelines when changes occur

4 Risk Management
The system automatically categorizes orders:
- **On Track** (Green): Projected to finish before deadline with buffer time
- **At Risk** (Amber): Projected completion is close to the deadline
- **Late** (Red): Projected completion is after the deadline


5 What you need to know
- **Update Task Status Regularly**: Keep your task statuses current so the system can accurately calculate timelines
- **Respect Dependencies**: Tasks with dependencies can't start until their prerequisites are complete
- **Check Critical Path**: Tasks highlighted in red are on the critical path - any delay here affects the deadline
- **Log Your Time**: Accurate time tracking helps improve future project estimates
